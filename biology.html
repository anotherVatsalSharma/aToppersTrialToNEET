Biology

What is Biology?

Many people believe that Biology is a subject which needs a lot of mugging up......but that is so far from the truth. Biology is not only about learning facts, but also about understanding them.

Biology is the natural science that studies life and living organisms, including their physical structure, chemical processes, molecular interactions, physiological mechanisms, development and evolution.

It is a beautiful, fascinating and mind-blowing subject that wows the learner as they progress.

What does Biology require?

As NEET aspirants, Biology should be your forte and you should aim to at least score full in it.

You would be glad to know that Biology has NO prerequisites. It only requires the person to have an open mind and a will to learn. Kudos!

But be ready for some mind-boggling concepts and some facts that may be difficult to understand at first.

Remember your patience will be tested.
